Lasagne

Description

A colorful, tasty dish. Served with garlic bread, this vegetarian lasagne makes an excellent meal for guests

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 (16 ounce) can tomato sauce
  • 1 (6 ounce) can tomato paste
  • 1 (14.5 ounce) can diced tomatoes
  • 2 teaspoons dried basil leaves
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 12 lasagne noodles
  • 16 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 3/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large skillet, cook the ground beef, onion, and garlic over medium heat until browned. Drain any excess fat.
  3. Add the tomato sauce, tomato paste, diced tomatoes, basil, salt, Italian seasoning, and black pepper to the skillet. Simmer for about 30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  4. While the sauce is simmering, bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook the lasagne noodles according to package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  5. In a mixing bowl, combine the ricotta cheese, 1 cup of mozzarella cheese, and Parmesan cheese.
  6. Spread a thin layer of the meat sauce on the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  7. Place a layer of lasagne noodles over the sauce.
  8. Spread a portion of the ricotta cheese mixture over the noodles.
  9. Sprinkle with a layer of shredded mozzarella cheese.
  10. Repeat the layering process (sauce, noodles, ricotta mixture, mozzarella) until all ingredients are used, finishing with a layer of sauce and a generous topping of mozzarella cheese.
  11. Cover the dish with aluminum foil and bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes.
  12. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes, or until the cheese is bubbly and golden brown.
  13. Let the lasagne cool for about 15 minutes before serving to allow it to set.
