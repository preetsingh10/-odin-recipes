Ingredients

  • 2-3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-2 red chilies, sliced (adjust to your heat preference)
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 carrot, julienne
  • 1/2 cup snap peas
  • 1/2 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 1 cup protein of choice (chicken, shrimp, tofu) >

Steps

  1. Prepare the Noodles: Cook the noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Make the Sauce: In a small bowl, mix together soy sauce, oyster sauce, fish sauce, dark soy sauce, sugar, and chili paste/flakes.
  3. Stir-fry: Heat a large pan or wok over medium-high heat. Add a bit of oil and stir-fry the garlic and red chilies until fragrant.
  4. Add Protein: Add your chosen protein and cook until done.
  5. Add Vegetables: Add the vegetables and stir-fry until they are tender-crisp.
  6. Combine: Add the cooked noodles and sauce to the pan. Toss everything together until the noodles are well coated with the sauce.
  7. Add Basil: Stir in the fresh basil leaves just before serving, allowing them to wilt slightly.
  8. Serve: Serve hot, garnished with lime wedges, chopped peanuts, fresh cilantro, and green onions if desired.
